Privacy Policy

For the StudentCC Application

Last Updated: September 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudentCC, a mobile application developed by Salman Malvasi. This Privacy Policy outlines our commitment to protecting your privacy and explains how we handle your information when you use the App to access VIT Chennai's VTOP portal.

Our guiding principle is to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide a great user experience. Your data is your own, and this App is designed to be a secure window to your academic information, not a tool for data collection.

Your Login Credentials Are Safe

We do not store, view, or transmit your VTOP username or password. Your credentials are used only to log in to the VTOP portal directly from your device and are never saved on any server or seen by the developer.

2. Information We Collect

To function, the App needs to access certain types of information. We categorize this into two groups:

Academic Information (Fetched from VTOP)

When you log in, the App fetches your academic data directly from the official VTOP portal. This information is stored **locally and securely on your device** and includes:

  • Your name, registration number, and profile details.
  • Course information, timetable, and schedules.
  • Attendance records.
  • Grades, marks, and GPA/CGPA details.

Anonymous Usage Data (For App Improvement)

We use **Firebase Analytics** to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how the App is used. This helps us understand which features are popular, identify crashes, and improve performance. This data is **not linked to you personally** and includes:

  • General usage events (e.g., screen views, button clicks).
  • Device information (e.g., OS version, device model).
  • Anonymized location data (e.g., country).

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core App Functionality: To display your timetable, attendance, grades, and allow you to use features like the GPA calculator.
  • To Improve the Application: To analyze anonymous usage data to identify bugs, make performance improvements, and develop new features that students find useful.
  • To Send Notifications: If you grant permission, the App may use your schedule information to send you timely reminders for classes or exams.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your information seriously.

  • On-Device Storage: All your personal academic information is stored in a secure, private database on your device only. It is not uploaded to any external server.
  • Data Encryption: All communication between the App and the VTOP portal is encrypted using standard HTTPS protocols.
  • Data Deletion: You have full control over your data. You can clear all locally stored information at any time through the App's settings.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personally identifiable academic information with any third parties. Anonymous usage data is shared with Google (as part of Firebase Analytics) for processing, but this data cannot be used to identify you.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and View all your data stored within the App.
  • Delete all your locally stored data by clearing the App's cache or reinstalling it.
  • Opt-out of anonymous analytics tracking through the App's settings (if available).

StudentCC is an open-source project, meaning its source code is publicly available for review and contribution. This ensures full transparency in how the App handles your data. The App is an enhanced version based on the original "VTOP Chennai" app developed by Sujit Kumar, to whom we extend our sincere gratitude.
